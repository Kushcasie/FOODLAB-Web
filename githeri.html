Kenyan Style Githeri

Ingredients

  • 300g boiled maize
  • 200g boiled beans
  • 3 tomatoes diced
  • 1 large onion diced/pureed
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 1 tablespoon curry powder
  • 1 tablespoon food masala
  • 2 beef boulion cube
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 green/red bell pepper (optional)
  • Corriander (Dhania) chopped

Instructions

  1. Heat your sufuria on a medium heat and then add the vegetable oil followed by the onions and cook them until they just turn translucent.
  2. Add the ginger and garlic and cook for about a minute and then add the curry powder and the food masala, which will cook for a minute to cook off the raw taste and aroma.
  3. Add diced/pureed tomatoes and beef boulion cubes, stir, cover and reduce heat to a low simmer. Cook for about 5minutes or until the mixture reduces and thickens.
  4. Add the maize and beans,salt and freshly cracked pepper to taste.
  5. Cover and simmer for about 10 minutes for the sauce to thicken and all the flavors to come together.
  6. Finally, add the dhania and bell pepper and simmer for just a minute. Turn off the heat and serve.
