Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 1 cup of Rice
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 or 2 Garlic Clove diced (depending on preference)
  • 1/2 tsp Tomato paste
  • 1 Royco Chicken/Beef Bouillon Cube
  • 1 Small red bell pepper (diced)(optional)
  • 1 Small green Bell Pepper (diced)(optional)
  • 1/2 Small onion (diced)
  • 2 cups boiled Yellow beans

Instructions

  1. First, clean the rice by rinsing or washing the rice under cold water to remove excess starch.
  2. Add the rice to a bowl & rinse under cold water until the water is clear.
  3. Add the rice to a bowl & rinse under cold water until the water is clear.
  4. On medium high heat in a pot add the cooking oil.
  5. Once the oil becomes hot add the onion and bell peppers, and saut√© for 3 to 5 minutes.
  6. Add fresh minced garlic and saut√© for 1 to 2 minutes or until it becomes fragrant.
  7. Add the dry seasoning mixture to the veggies and stir everything together.
  8. Add the tomato paste, stir and allow it to dissolve.
  9. Next, add the boiled yellow beans and stir.
  10. Add the 2 cups of water and the 1 cup of washed and rinsed rice to the pot.
  11. Allow the liquid to come to a boil.
  12. Allow the water to cook down for a little bit, then lower the temperature to low heat.
  13. Cover the pot with a lid. (You can do the aluminum foil method and allow the rice to steam).
  14. Let the rice steam or cook for 20-25 minutes.
  15. Once the rice is done, use a fork to fluff the rice.
  16. Serve while hot. Enjoy!
